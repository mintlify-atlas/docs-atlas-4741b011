## Welcome to BeanQuick!

BeanQuick is your go-to platform for discovering local coffee shops and placing orders for pickup. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know as a customer.

---

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Navigate to the registration page and create your customer account:
    
    - Provide your name, email, and password
    - Verify your email address by clicking the activation link sent to your inbox
    - Once verified, you can log in to your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Available Cafes">
    After logging in, you'll land on the Customer Dashboard where you can:
    
    - View all active coffee shops in your area
    - See which shops are currently open (indicated by green "Abierta ahora" badge)
    - Use the search bar to find specific cafes by name
    - Check ratings for each establishment (displayed with star icons)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore a Shop's Menu">
    Click on any cafe card to visit their store page and browse their full product catalog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browsing & Ordering

### Viewing a Cafe's Store

When you enter a cafe's store page (`/tienda/:id`), you'll see:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Store Header" icon="store">
    - Business logo and banner image
    - Store name and address
    - Open/closed status indicator
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Product Filters" icon="filter">
    - Search bar to find products by name
    - Category dropdown to filter by product type
    - Real-time filtering as you type
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Product Information

Each product card displays:

- **Product image**: High-quality photo of the item
- **Name and description**: What you're ordering
- **Price**: Listed in local currency
- **Rating**: Average customer rating with star display
- **Stock availability**: Shows remaining quantity
- **Add to cart button**: Disabled if out of stock or store is closed

<Warning>
  Products that are out of stock will show an "AGOTADO" badge and cannot be added to cart. If you've already added the maximum available stock to your cart, you'll see a "LÍMITE EN CARRITO" badge.
</Warning>

### Adding Items to Cart

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Products">
    Click the "Agregar" button on any product to add it to your cart. The floating cart icon will automatically open to show your items.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Cart Quantities">
    In the floating cart panel:
    
    - Use `+` and `-` buttons to adjust quantities
    - Click the trash icon to remove items
    - View real-time total price updates
    - See how many items are in your cart
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Proceed to Checkout">
    When ready, click "Ir a Checkout" to finalize your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Placing an Order

### Checkout Process

On the checkout page (`/cliente/checkout`):

1. **Review your order**: See all products, quantities, and prices
2. **Enter delivery address**: Provide the address where you'll pick up your order
3. **Confirm payment**: Click "Confirmar y Pagar" to submit your order

<Info>
  Once confirmed, your order is sent to the business immediately. You'll receive an order confirmation and can track its status in real-time.
</Info>

### Order States

Your order will progress through these stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pagado (Paid)">
    Your order has been received and payment confirmed. The business will begin preparing your items shortly.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preparando (Preparing)">
    The cafe is actively preparing your order. This is when your coffee is being made!
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Listo (Ready)">
    Your order is complete and ready for pickup at the specified address.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Entregado (Delivered)">
    You've picked up your order. You can now leave a review!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Orders

### Viewing Order History

Access your order history from the navigation menu by clicking "Mis Pedidos" (`/cliente/mis-pedidos`).

**Features:**

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Order Filters" icon="funnel">
    Filter orders by status:
    - Todos (All)
    - Pendiente (Pending)
    - Preparando (Preparing)
    - Listo (Ready)
    - Entregado (Delivered)
    - Cancelado (Cancelled)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Order Details" icon="list">
    Each order shows:
    - Order number and cafe logo
    - Status badge with color coding
    - Pickup time
    - Total price
    - Business address
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Expanding Order Details

Click on any order to expand and view:

- Full product list with images
- Quantity for each item
- Individual product prices
- Delivery address

### Canceling Orders

You can cancel orders that are in "Pendiente" (Pending) status:

1. Click on the order to expand details
2. Click the "Cancelar Pedido" button
3. Confirm the cancellation in the popup dialog

<Warning>
  Once an order moves to "Preparando" status, it cannot be cancelled by customers. You'll need to contact the business directly.
</Warning>

## Rating Products & Leaving Reviews

### When Can You Rate?

You can rate products only after your order has been marked as "Entregado" (Delivered).

### How to Leave a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Delivered Order">
    Go to "Mis Pedidos" and find an order with "Entregado" status. Click to expand the order details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Calificar' Button">
    Each product in the order will have a "Calificar" button with a star icon. Click it to open the rating modal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rate the Product">
    In the rating modal:
    
    - Click on stars to select your rating (1-5 stars)
    - Add an optional comment describing your experience
    - Click "Enviar Calificación" to submit
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Once submitted, the button changes to "Calificado" with a checkmark, and you cannot rate the same product again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your ratings help other customers make informed decisions and help businesses improve their products!
</Info>

### Managing Your Reviews

View all your submitted reviews from "Mis Calificaciones" (`/cliente/mis-calificaciones`) in the navigation menu.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Add Items to Cart" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Possible reasons:**
    - Store is currently closed
    - Product is out of stock
    - You've already added maximum available quantity to cart
    
    **Solution:** Check the product badge and store status. Wait until the store opens or choose alternative products.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Order Not Showing Up" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Solution:** 
    - Refresh the "Mis Pedidos" page
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify you're logged into the correct account
    - Contact the business if the issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't Cancel Order" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Solution:** Orders can only be cancelled in "Pendiente" status. Once preparation begins, contact the business directly via the address shown in your order details.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't Submit Rating" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Solution:** Ratings can only be submitted for "Entregado" orders. Each product can only be rated once per order. If you've already rated a product, the button will show "Calificado."
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
